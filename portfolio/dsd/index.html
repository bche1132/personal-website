COVID-19 Decision Support Dashboard

This dashboard is intended to help governors, mayors, business owners, and other community-based decision makers answer their COVID-19 questions. These questions are inspired by the National Governors Association’s Roadmap to Recovery Report and will be a growing list as more data is assembled. Use the map to find information about COVID-19 cases for your area of interest. You can also add multiple locations to the dashboard to be able to easily compare COVID-19 information for those areas.
