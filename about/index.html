Hi there! You can call me Bri!

My Journey into UX:

  1. Earned a Bachelor's degree in Art, specializing in oil painting
  2. Explored interior design for a brief period
  3. Completed a Master's in Computer Science with a focus on Game Design
  4. Began my career as a Graphics & Visualization Software Engineer in a research and innovation company
  5. Discovered my passion for blending development and design—leading me to become a UX Engineer

What I Do Day-to-Day

No two days are the same! I thrive in a fast-paced environment, managing multiple projects while balancing strategic planning with adaptability. Working across diverse industries and customer needs means constantly learning, evolving, and embracing new technologies.

A typical day might involve:

  • Reviewing wireframes and refining user flows
  • Designing high-fidelity mockups
  • Conducting user research and interviews
  • Developing front-end components to bring designs to life
  • Refining product templates or drafting new logos

Beyond my role as a UX Engineer, I also lead a team—ensuring they are engaged in meaningful work where they can create impact.

Why UX?

UX combines my love for creativity and technology. It allows me to design functional, innovative experiences while advocating for the needs of real users. For me, it’s about crafting intuitive, impactful solutions that bridge the gap between design and development.
